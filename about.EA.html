Welcome to The Extant Alchemyst.

Your repository of the ars arcanum, both ancient and contemporary. Alchemy is often defined as the attempt to transmute lead into gold, or the medieval precursor to modern chemistry. While the latter is accurate enough, the former is actually a very misunderstood analogy. The ancients believed the human spirit to be malleable. Through study, meditation, and a metaphoricized application of the four elements to induce change in nature, man could transmute his soul from a heavy, lackluster, lead-lump into a more shining, conducive, golden aura. They called it the 'Great Work'. Here, at The Extant Alchemyst we interpret Alchemy the same way and host products to help you achieve your Magnum Opus.
